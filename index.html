    How to play

    The Everything Exhibition generates virtual 3D exhibitions for any topics you want. Enter a topic in the box at the top and click "Generate!". You can also pick another language.

    Controls

    arrow keys/WASD: move

    Hold shift: run

    Q/E: fly down/up

    Click on blue links: jump to another topic

    Multiplayer

    "New group" button: create a multiplayer group and put a link to it in your clipboard

    "Invite" button: put a link to your current group in your clipboard

    You can set your nickname, your face as text, as well as your color in the sidebar.
