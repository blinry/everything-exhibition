This is you:

    Generate virtual 3D exhibitions from Wikipedia for any topic you want! Some starting points: Hedgehog Optical illusion Subatomic particle You can also enter your own topic and click "Generate", click the "Random" button to be surprised.

    This application is multi-lingual, you can pick any language from the dropdown at the left, or try one of these: Burrito Surströmming ラーメン

    We recommend using the Chrome browser.

    Move around

    Click into the 3D space to start moving, and press Escape to release your mouse.

    • Arrow keys/WASD: Walk around
    • Hold shift: Move faster
    • Q/E: Fly down/up
    • Left click on blue links: Jump to another topic
    Edit and scribble
    • Right click on content: Edit the content and make Wikipedia better!
    • Left mouse button: Draw on walls. Your drawings are shared within your group.
    • C: clear drawings
    Invite your friends!
    • "New group" button: Create a multiplayer group and put a link to it in your clipboard
    • "Invite" button: Put a link to your current group in your clipboard
    • You can set your nickname, your face as text, as well as your color in the sidebar.
